Math Rules

1. Bps Math

  • Percents: Multiply first digit, drop level, add 1 zero. (Percent drops 2 places).
  • Bps: Multiply, drop a level, drop 1 zero. (Basis point drops 4 places).
  • Benchmarks: 1k is 10 bps of 1m; 1m is 10 bps of 1b.

2. Big Math

  • Levels: Thousand (1), Million (2), Billion (3).
  • Scaling: 150k ppl √ó 100k: Up 1 level for k, 2 for m. Multiply front digits and add zero places (2 for 100, 1 for 10).
  • Double Digit: XX √ó YY multiplication.
