Math Shortcuts

1. Bps Math

  • Percents: Multiply first digit, drop a level (e.g., Bil to Mil), add 1 zero.
  • Bps: Multiply, drop a level, drop 1 zero.
  • Scale: 1k is 10 bps of 1m; 1m is 10 bps of 1b.

2. Big Math

  • Levels: k (1), m (2), b (3).
  • Salary Math: For 150k ppl √ó 100k: Up 1 level for k, 2 for m. Multiply front digits and add zero places.
  • Double Digits: XX √ó YY.
